Hobby Projects

Tiles Library

I was building my website, and wanted to add tiles as my main interface. I searched for a good library for tiles, and not finding anything to my liking, decided to make my own. After much work, I had what I wanted. I then thought if I already have all the code, why not make it open-source and help someone else in their search for the right tile library. So if you want to check it out, here is the Tiles library. :)
